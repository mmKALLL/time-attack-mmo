RPG DESIGN DRAFT · TURN 1

Dungeon Combat — three visual directions

A live look at the signature stuck-combat mechanic: the party touched a Cave Slime and the fight snowballed into an irregular 9-unit block — players and enemies locked together, both sides auto-casting every 1.5 s (square timers), with attack-shape highlights (ember 3×1, violet 2×2) and floating damage. Same scene, three renderings of the sprite / lighting style. Reference an option in chat by its badge — 1a, 1b, 1c.

1a

Emberdeep

warm · crisp · classic

Warm torch-lit stone, saturated colors, sharp 1 px outlines. The cozy Ragnarok-dungeon warmth — inviting for long grind sessions.

1b

Brightsteel

bright · chunky · punchy

Higher exposure, bold 2 px outlines, extra saturation. Sprites read clearest at small size — the MapleStory-leaning, arcade-legible pick.

1c

Gloomlight

moody · desaturated · dark

Desaturated palette, heavy vignette, cool violet ambient. The truest "dark-mode default" — atmospheric and tense.
